Creamy Raspberry and Blackberry Smoothie

Per Serving: 195 calories; protein 5.8g; carbohydrates 42.5g; fat 1.7g; cholesterol 4.3mg; sodium 58.6mg.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 6 ounce vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Put the berries, ice, yogurt, honey and banana in the blender
  2. Blend it to the moon.
  3. Enjoy!
